What is meteor shower exactly?

Meteor showers are caused by streams of cosmic debris called meteoroids entering Earth's atmosphere at extremely high speeds on parallel trajectories.

Meteor shower have different parrents. The majority come from comets, which include ”C comet” (Non-Periodic comets) and “P comet” (Periodic comets), while a small number from asteroids. Meteoroids are the debris they leave in their orbits.

Where do meteoroids come from?

Different parent objects of meteoroids have different types of meteor shower in the universe.
